About Us

GROWMORE LIMITED is working as the Architectural and Structural Consultant for more than 10 Years. Our creative and innovative design concepts rank us as the Best Architectural Firm in Dhaka, Bangladesh. We always believe in efficient customer supports and unique design ideas in every aspects of our engagements.

GROWMORE

GROWMORE LIMITED was established in 2012 as a partnership firm with the name of GROWMORE Engineers & Architects. A group of professionals were willing to pool their resources to define a new order of Engineering Standard. Later in 2014, it has incorporated as a Private Limited company to the Registrar of Joint Stock Companies of Bangladesh under the Companies Act-1994.

Today, it is a fast growing Company for Building Design, Architectural and Structural Consultancy, Soil Test, Digital Survey, RAJUK Plan Pass, Civil Construction & Project Management. So far GROWMORE's integrity and technological resources comprise modern & world class solutions to meet the needs of today. Our Mission is to offer excellent service in each of our professional disciplines, in accordance with state of the art practices, codes of conduct and integrity.
